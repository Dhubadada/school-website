About Our School

Our Legacy

Since 1930, Prabartak Bidyapith has stood as a pillar of education in Chittagong. Our approach blends academic excellence with moral values to shape future-ready citizens. It continues to uphold its legacy with a focus on inclusive and value-based education, ensuring a brighter future for all its students.

  • ✔ Experienced and passionate faculty
  • ✔ Modern, student-focused teaching methods
  • ✔ Commitment to character building and leadership

Our Facilities

Science Lab

Our state-of-the-art laboratories provide hands-on experience in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Students can explore real-world experiments and develop scientific thinking.

Library

A vast collection of academic, reference, and story books supports our students' reading habits and research capabilities. A peaceful environment encourages learning and reflection.

Sports

We offer a full playground and indoor games for physical development and teamwork. Regular tournaments and physical training are integral to our curriculum.

Computer Lab

Equipped with modern PCs and high-speed internet, our computer lab teaches programming, graphics, typing, and ICT skills, enabling students to thrive in the digital age.
